What is Dell’s return policy?

You can return products that you bought directly from us within 15 days set by our Return Policy - https://www.dell.com/learn/au/en/aucorp1/policies-returns. If your order has an error, or if you want to return your order for another reason, don’t refuse delivery of your order. Refusing delivery may increase the amount of time that it takes to credit your account. If you would like to return your order, you should accept the delivery, contact our chat sales team or customer service team to arrange your return.

Where can I check the status of my Dell Order ?

Kindly visit https://www.dell.com/support/order-status/en-au/order-support for most updated status regarding your orders

What is the warranty on refurbished products ?

All Dell Outlet systems are sold with the same standard limited warranties as we offer on our new systems. And that’s not all! You can also upgrade warranty on your purchased Outlet Systems by selecting upgrade warranty option at while checking out.
